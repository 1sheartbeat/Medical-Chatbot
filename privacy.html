Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Information We Collect

When you use MediAssist, we collect the following information:

  • Conversation Data: The messages you send and the responses generated by the chatbot
  • Session Information: A unique session ID to maintain conversation continuity
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for analytics
  • Feedback: Any ratings or comments you provide about the responses

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the chatbot service
  • To maintain conversation history for continuity
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve response quality
  • To ensure system security and prevent abuse
  • To conduct research on chatbot performance (anonymized data only)

3. Data Storage

Your conversation data is stored in a secure database. We retain conversation history for:

  • Providing continuity in your conversations
  • Quality improvement and research purposes
  • System debugging and performance analysis

You can clear your conversation history at any time using the "Clear History" button in the chat interface.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Secure session management
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls for system administrators

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: MediAssist is an AI-powered informational tool only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice. The information provided should not be used as a substitute for consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.

Do not use this service for medical emergencies. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call emergency services immediately.

6. Third-Party Services

MediAssist may use third-party services for:

  • Large language model inference (Llama 3 8B)
  • Database hosting (if applicable)

These third parties may have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your conversation history
  • Delete your conversation history
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Opt out of data collection (by not using the service)

8. Children's Privacy

MediAssist is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
